A convenient feature of Snap!Clerk is the ability to upload receipts via email. Simply attach a receipt image to an email and send it to a unique, secure email address linked to your Skyclerk account. The image will be processed just like any receipt you upload using the Snap!Clerk mobile phone or web interface.

Visit https://app.skyclerk.com/snapclerk to obtain your Snap!Clerk email address (see below). If it is ever compromised by a spammer, just email help@skyclerk.com and we will create a new Snap!Clerk email address for you.

Currently Snap!Clerk accepts only images of receipts, which are analyzed and accurately entered into your Skyclerk ledger. The subject of your email is entered as the ledger entry note.

Soon you will also be able to submit receipts as PDF files and text emails.
