Skyclerk supports many different currencies. Currently, you can only update your currency via our web application (mobile does not support currency settings). To select your preferred currency please following the directions below.

1) Click on the Settings link in the upper right-hand corner.

2) Click the Edit link within the Currency section.

3) Update your preferred currency and press the "Save Changes" button.
